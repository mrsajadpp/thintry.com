WHO WE ARE

    Found By Few, a forward-thinking recruitment partner for Design and Product.

By putting people (not algorithms) at the front and centre of everything we do, we help your business attract and retain the world’s best talent in Product Management and Design.

Talk to us

We are an optimistic and friendly group of Product and Design recruiters who are passionate about what we do, who we place and who with.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

We seek to champion diversity and inclusion in our office, our community and with our partners.

It’s no secret that diverse and inclusive companies are better able to win and retain top talent than companies who are not. Perhaps you’ve already realised the value of DEI and don’t know where to start or maybe you’ve been struggling to recruit and retain diverse candidates.

Maybe you just want to partner with a specialist Product and Design recruitment company that genuinely shares your values.

Whether it’s candidate anonymisation technology, hiring process audit or just ongoing friendly and frank advice. Let us know how we can help.
