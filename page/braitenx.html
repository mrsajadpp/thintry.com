Thintry Clarifies: No Connection to Braitenx - Our Services Remain Unique


No Connection to Braitenx


Thintry is committed to providing innovative and original design solutions to our clients. Here at Thintry, we value creativity and ethical business practices.

Recently, some confusion has arisen regarding a company called Braitenx. We want to be clear: Thintry has no affiliation with Braitenx.

Thintry was founded in 2020 by Muhammed Sajad PP and Adil Nasar Kc, a passionate designer with a vision to offer a comprehensive design service. Since then, Thintry has grown, establishing its headquarters in Calicut and expanding its services to include graphic design, web development, branding, and more.

We understand that some similarities might exist between Thintry and Braitenx. This might be because a former employee of Thintry is associated with Braitenx. However, it’s important to understand that this individual’s departure from Thintry was due to a misalignment with our core values, specifically the originality and ethical foundation upon which Thintry is built.

Thintry remains dedicated to providing our clients with unique and effective design solutions. We are constantly evolving and pushing boundaries to stay at the forefront of the design industry.

If you have any questions or concerns about Thintry's services or our commitment to originality, please don't hesitate to contact us.

We look forward to collaborating with you on your next design project!
